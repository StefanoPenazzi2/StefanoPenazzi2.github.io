$$ 
\min_{\theta} \max_{\omega} F(\theta, \omega) = \min_{\theta} \max_{\omega} \left( \mathbb{E}_{x \sim P} \left[g_f(V_\omega(x | y))\right] + \mathbb{E}_{x \sim Q_\theta} \left[\log(-g_f(V_\omega(x | y)))\right] \right) + 1 
$$
